Before writing any code, produce a plan:

1. What files are involved and what does each own?
2. What is the smallest safe change that achieves the goal?
3. What is the implementation order? (dependencies first)
4. What could go wrong, and what's the fallback?
5. What tests or checks will confirm it's correct?

Be specific. Name the files. Name the functions. Name the tests.
